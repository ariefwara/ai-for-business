# Agentic Enterprise

This series explores how organizations are moving from AI tools and copilots toward the **agentic enterprise**: an operating model where AI agents can understand context, take action across enterprise systems, collaborate with humans, and remain under clear governance controls.

Use this page as your main index. If you're just starting out, read articles 1 through 12. If you're building a platform, begin with articles 13 through 21. If your focus is on organizational change, start with articles 22 through 33.
